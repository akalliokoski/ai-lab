# Diagnostic Accuracy of a Portable Electromyography and Electrocardiography Device to Measure Sleep Bruxism in a Sleep Apnea Population: A Comparative Study

Source: PubMed / Clocks & Sleep (2023)
PMID: 37987398
DOI: 10.3390/clockssleep5040047

## Abstract-style summary
- Compared PSG expert scoring against manual and automatic analysis from a portable EMG+EKG device in 22 subjects with suspected OSA.
- Motivated the study by noting that post-apnea hypermotor activity can be mistaken for sleep bruxism when using portable EMG alone.
- Shows that event scoring logic and context matter when portable EMG is used in realistic populations.
