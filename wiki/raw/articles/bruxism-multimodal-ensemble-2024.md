# Computational ensemble expert system classification for the recognition of bruxism using physiological signals

Source: Europe PMC / Heliyon (2024)
PMCID: PMC10881886
DOI: 10.1016/j.heliyon.2024.e25958

## Abstract-style summary
- Uses the CAP Sleep Database and combines EEG, ECG, and EMG-derived features.
- The paper reports EEG-only accuracy around 95%, ECG-only around 96%, EMG-stage-combined around 98%, and an ensemble model around 99%.
- Signals were summarized using power spectral density features and classified with Weka ensemble methods.

## Important caveat
The data are again tiny and likely split at the epoch level instead of subject level. This makes the results useful for feature-pipeline reproduction, but not a trustworthy estimate of generalization.
