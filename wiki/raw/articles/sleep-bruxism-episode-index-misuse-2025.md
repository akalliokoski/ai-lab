# Moving beyond bruxism episode index: Discarding misuse of the number of sleep bruxism episodes as masticatory muscle pain biomarker

Source: PubMed / Journal of Sleep Research (2025)
PMID: 39134874
DOI: 10.1111/jsr.14301

## Abstract-style summary
- Video-polysomnography study in 220 adults evaluated whether bruxism episode index predicts masticatory muscle pain intensity.
- Correlation and regression analyses did not support the episode index as a useful biomarker for muscle pain intensity.
- The paper argues against over-interpreting a single episode-count summary as the clinically meaningful representation.

## Takeaway for ai-lab
This is direct evidence against collapsing pass41 into one crude episode-rate feature. If the repo moves toward events, it should keep a compact multi-feature event block rather than replacing the whole representation with a single count.
